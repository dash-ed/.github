## Project DASHED (DAta Science High school EDucation)

Materials and resources for Project DASHED, a collaborative research project between University of Auckland and Paderborn University, with a focus on preparing high school students for a data science future by developing innovative teaching methods at the interface of statistics and computer science education.

Project DASHED was funded by the German Federal Ministry of Education and Research, the German Academic Exchange Service, and Education New Zealand (Manapou ki te Ao) through the Programme for Project-Related Personal Exchange (a NZ-German academic partnership).
